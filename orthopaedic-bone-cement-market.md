<p><p>The orthopaedic bone cement market is categorized into low, medium, and high viscosity cements, each designed for specific surgical applications. Low viscosity cements are fluid and ideal for precise applications in bone voids. Medium viscosity cements strike a balance, offering good flow and stability for various procedures. High viscosity cements are thicker, providing enhanced mechanical strength and stability, making them suitable for load-bearing applications. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing fixation and enhancing patient outcomes in orthopedic surgeries.</p></p>
