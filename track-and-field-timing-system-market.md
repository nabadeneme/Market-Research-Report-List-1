<p><strong>The Track and Field Timing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line-scan System</li><li>Full-frame System</li><li>Break-beam Timing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Track and Field Timing System market consists of several key types. The line-scan system uses a single line of pixels to capture images, providing high-speed results for sprint events. Full-frame systems capture entire images at once, suitable for various track and field formats but at a lower speed. Break-beam timing systems utilize beams of light to detect runners’ finish times as they cross the beam, offering instant results and high accuracy, often employed for more straightforward competitions.</p></p>
