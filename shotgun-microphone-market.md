<p><p>A shotgun microphone is a highly directional microphone designed to capture audio from a specific source while minimizing background noise. Its long, narrow pickup pattern allows it to focus on sounds coming from a particular direction, making it ideal for film, television, and field recording applications. The design typically features a highly directional capsule mounted on a long, tubular body, often used in conjunction with a boom pole or mounted on cameras.</p><p>The Shotgun Microphone Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period.
