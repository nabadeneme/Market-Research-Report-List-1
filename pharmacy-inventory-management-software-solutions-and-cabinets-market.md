Centralized dispensing systems streamline medication management by centralizing inventory control, enhancing efficiency, and reducing errors at a central pharmacy location. Conversely, decentralized dispensing systems distribute medication management closer to the point of care, improving accessibility and patient safety. Each system offers unique advantages, with centralized systems focusing on bulk management and cost-effectiveness, while decentralized systems prioritize timely medication delivery and individualized patient care.</p></p>
