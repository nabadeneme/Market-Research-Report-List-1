<p><p>The automotive air quality sensor market comprises two main types: double sensors and triple sensors. Double sensors typically detect two key pollutants, such as carbon monoxide and volatile organic compounds, providing essential air quality monitoring for vehicle occupants. In contrast, triple sensors enhance detection capabilities by measuring three pollutants, often including nitrogen dioxide alongside the others. This advanced monitoring ensures a more comprehensive understanding of the air quality inside the vehicle, leading to improved health and comfort for passengers.</p></p>
