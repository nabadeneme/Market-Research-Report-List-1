<p><strong>The Reaction Hydro Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mixed Flow Hydro Turbine</li><li>Axial Flow Hydro Turbine</li><li>Inclinedflow Hydro Turbine</li><li>Tubular Hydro Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reaction hydro turbine market includes various types designed for specific flow conditions. Mixed flow hydro turbines combine axial and radial flows, making them versatile for varied water levels. Axial flow hydro turbines direct water parallel to the turbine shaft, suitable for low head applications. Inclined flow hydro turbines operate with water flowing at an angle, optimizing efficiency in certain scenarios. Tubular hydro turbines, with their cylindrical shape, are effective in large flow areas, particularly for low-head sites. Each type caters to specific operational needs and efficiencies.</p></p>
