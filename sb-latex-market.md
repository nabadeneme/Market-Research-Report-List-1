<p><strong>The SB Latex Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion Styrene Butadiene Latex</li><li>Solution Styrene Butadiene Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SB latex market includes two primary types: emulsion styrene butadiene latex and solution styrene butadiene latex. Emulsion styrene butadiene latex is produced through a polymerization process in water, offering excellent adhesion and durability, making it ideal for coatings and adhesives. In contrast, solution styrene butadiene latex is created by dissolving styrene butadiene rubber in organic solvents, which is commonly used in applications like sealants and paints. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs across various sectors.</p></p>
