<p><p>An Autogenous Grinding Mill (AG mill) is a type of grinding mill that uses the material itself to assist in the grinding process, primarily for ore processing. These mills are particularly effective for large-scale mining operations that require high throughput and lower operational expenses.</p><p>The Autogenous Grinding Mill Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for minerals and metals worldwide.
