<p><ul><li>Cage Rotor</li><li>Wound Rotor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed induction motor market is categorized into cage rotor and wound rotor types. Cage rotor motors, characterized by their simple design and robustness, are widely used for applications requiring high efficiency at low maintenance. They are favored in industrial settings for their reliability. Wound rotor motors, on the other hand, offer enhanced control over torque and speed, making them ideal for high-torque applications. Their ability to connect additional resistance allows for better performance under varying load conditions.</p></p>
