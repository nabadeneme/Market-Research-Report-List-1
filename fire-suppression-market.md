<p><p>The fire suppression market encompasses various types, including dry chemical fire suppression, foam fire suppression, and CO2 fire suppression systems. Dry chemical systems use a powder to interrupt the chemical reaction of fires, making them effective for flammable liquids and gases. Foam systems create a barrier to smother flames, ideal for liquid fires. CO2 fire suppression displaces oxygen to extinguish fires, suitable for delicate equipment environments. Other methods may include water mist and inert gas systems, each tailored to specific fire risks.</p></p>
