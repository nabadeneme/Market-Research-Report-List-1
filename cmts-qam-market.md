<p><p>The CMTS (Cable Modem Termination System) market features primarily two types: Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS) and Modular CMTS (M-CMTS). I-CMTS combines all functionalities into a single unit, simplifying deployment and maintenance, making it suitable for smaller networks. Conversely, M-CMTS offers a modular approach, allowing operators to scale based on demand by adding or removing components independently. This flexibility is ideal for larger networks with varying traffic loads, as it supports evolving technologies and bandwidth requirements.</p></p>
