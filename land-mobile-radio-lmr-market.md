<p><p>Land Mobile Radio (LMR) refers to wireless communication systems used primarily by public safety and industrial sectors for reliable voice and data transmission. The VHF range (40MHz – 174MHz) is favored for its long-range capabilities and better penetration in rural areas. UHF (200MHz – 512MHz) offers shorter range but better urban penetration and is widely adopted for commercial use. The SHF range (700MHz – 1000MHz) supports high-data-rate applications and modern digital communication, enhancing interoperability and network efficiency in various environments.</p></p>
