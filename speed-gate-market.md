<p><ul><li>Single Movement</li><li>Double Movement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Speed gate markets can be categorized into two types: Single Movement and Double Movement. </p><p>Single Movement speed gates operate with one directional swing, allowing passage in only one way, providing efficient access control in high-traffic areas. </p><p>In contrast, Double Movement speed gates feature dual directional swings, permitting entry and exit simultaneously. This design maximizes throughput and flexibility, making them ideal for environments requiring quick and efficient movement, such as airports, offices, or event venues, while maintaining security and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
