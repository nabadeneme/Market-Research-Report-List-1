<p><ul><li>Solvent Based Adhesives</li><li>Solvent-free Adhesives</li><li>Water Based Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible packaging lamination adhesives market comprises three main types: solvent-based adhesives, solvent-free adhesives, and water-based adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding but involve volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-free adhesives contain no solvents, making them eco-friendly and suitable for high-performance applications. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a carrier and are safer for the environment, though they may require specific substrates for optimal bonding. Each type serves various applications in the packaging industry, balancing performance, safety, and environmental impact.</p></p>
