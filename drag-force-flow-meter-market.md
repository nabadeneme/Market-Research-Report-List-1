<p><p>The drag force flow meter market is divided into two main types: mechanical and electronic. Mechanical flow meters use physical components like gears or rotors to measure fluid flow through drag force, often relying on simple moving parts. In contrast, electronic flow meters utilize sensors and advanced technology to capture and interpret fluid movement digitally, offering higher accuracy and real-time data. Both types serve various industries, with mechanical meters being more traditional and electronic meters increasingly favored for their precision and efficiency.</p></p>
