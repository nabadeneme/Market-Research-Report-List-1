<p><p>The polyurethane thermoset foam insulation material market is categorized into two main types: rigid and flexible. Rigid polyurethane foam offers excellent thermal insulation properties, making it ideal for applications like building insulation, refrigeration, and aerospace components. Its structural integrity provides durability and resistance to moisture. In contrast, flexible polyurethane foam is known for its cushioning and sound-absorbing qualities, typically used in furniture, automotive seating, and mattresses. Both types enhance energy efficiency and comfort in various industries.</p></p>
