<p><p>The industrial film market comprises various polymer types, each with distinct properties and applications. **LLDPE** offers flexibility and puncture resistance, ideal for stretch films. **LDPE** is known for its clarity and ease of processing, used in packaging. **HDPE** provides strength and moisture resistance, suitable for heavy-duty applications. **PET/BOPET** films are durable and heat-resistant, used in electronics and food packaging. **PP/BOPP** films are lightweight and printable, common in labels and laminates. **CPP** films are heat-sealable, used in food packaging. **PVC** is versatile and cost-effective, used in construction. **BOPA** offers high barrier properties, used in food and medical packaging. Others include niche polymers for specialized applications.</p></p>
