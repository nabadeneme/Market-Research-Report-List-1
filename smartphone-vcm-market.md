<p><p>The smartphone VCM (Voice Coil Motor) market can be categorized into two primary types: Ball Structure VCM and Shrapnel Structure VCM. Ball Structure VCM utilizes a spherical design that provides enhanced stability and precision in focus and image stabilization, making it ideal for high-end smartphone cameras.
