<p><p>The Emergency Nurse Call System market is divided into two main types: Wired Nurse Call Systems and Wireless Nurse Call Systems. Wired Nurse Call Systems rely on physical connections, offering stability and reliability in communication within healthcare facilities. They are often easier to integrate into existing infrastructure. Conversely, Wireless Nurse Call Systems utilize radio frequency or cellular technology, providing flexibility and mobility for healthcare staff. This enables rapid response times and enhances patient care by allowing nurses to be more mobile within the facility.</p></p>
