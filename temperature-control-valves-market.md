<p><p>The temperature control valves market encompasses various types, including thermostatic valves that automatically adjust flow based on temperature changes, and self-actuating valves that respond to pressure variations without external energy. Internally sensed valves monitor internal conditions to regulate temperature accurately, while electrically or pneumatically actuated valves utilize external power sources for precise control. Each type serves specific applications in HVAC, industrial processes, and automation, offering energy efficiency and enhanced control over temperature regulation in fluid systems.</p></p>
