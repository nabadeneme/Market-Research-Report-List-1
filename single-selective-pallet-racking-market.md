<p><p>Single selective pallet racking is a popular storage solution in warehouses, categorized into two types: roll form and structural steel. Roll form pallet racking features lightweight, easy-to-assemble frames made from cold-rolled steel, ideal for environments requiring flexibility and quick adjustments. In contrast, structural steel pallet racking utilizes heavier, robust materials, providing enhanced durability and load capacity, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types enable efficient space utilization, allowing for direct access to stored pallets, optimizing inventory management.</p></p>
