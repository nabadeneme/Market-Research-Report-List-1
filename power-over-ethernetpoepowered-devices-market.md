<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) powered devices market includes two primary segments: Powered Device Controllers and Powered Device ICs. Powered Device Controllers manage the power distribution to devices, ensuring they receive the correct voltage and current while adhering to PoE standards. Powered Device ICs, on the other hand, are integrated circuits that enable devices to communicate their power requirements and manage energy consumption efficiently. Together, these components facilitate the seamless integration of power and data transmission over Ethernet networks, enhancing network versatility.</p></p>
