<p><strong>The Neural Control Interface Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive</li><li>Noninvasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Neural Control Interface (NCI) market is categorized into invasive and non-invasive types. Invasive NCI involves surgical implantation of devices directly into the brain or neural tissue, providing high-resolution signals for applications like prosthetics control. Conversely, non-invasive NCI employs external devices, such as EEG caps or functional near-infrared spectroscopy (fNIRS), to record brain activity without surgery, making them safer and more accessible. Both types facilitate communication between the brain and external systems, benefiting medical, gaming, and rehabilitation sectors.</p></p>
