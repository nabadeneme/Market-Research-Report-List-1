<p><strong>The Commercial Energy Recovery Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensation Rotors</li><li>Hygroscopic Rotors</li><li>Sorption Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial energy recovery wheel market is segmented into three main types: condensation rotors, hygroscopic rotors, and sorption rotors. Condensation rotors transfer heat and moisture from exhaust air to incoming air, enhancing efficiency in humid conditions. Hygroscopic rotors utilize moisture-absorbing materials to optimize temperature and humidity control. Sorption rotors involve chemical processes that capture and release moisture, offering advanced energy recovery in diverse climates. Each type addresses specific energy efficiency needs in commercial HVAC systems, promoting sustainability and cost savings.</p></p>
