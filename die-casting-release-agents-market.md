<p><strong>The Die Casting Release Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based Release Agents</li><li>Water-free Release Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Die Casting Release Agents market includes Water-based and Water-free Release Agents. Water-based agents are eco-friendly, cost-effective, and easy to apply, using water as a carrier to reduce emissions and improve safety. Water-free agents, often solvent-based, offer superior lubrication and release properties, ideal for high-temperature applications and complex molds. Both types prevent sticking, enhance surface finish, and extend mold life, but water-free agents are preferred for precision casting due to their higher performance, while water-based options are favored for sustainability and lower environmental impact.</p></p>
