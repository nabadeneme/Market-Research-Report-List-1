Conversely, CHO-derived vaccines are generated from Chinese hamster ovary cells, offering higher yield and scalability for mass production. Each type has distinct advantages in terms of manufacturing efficiency, cost, and immunogenic response, shaping market preferences and influencing vaccination strategies worldwide.</p></p>
