<p><p>The refrigeration dryer market can be categorized into two main types: continuous and intermittent. Continuous refrigeration dryers operate without interruptions, providing a constant supply of dry air, making them ideal for applications with steady demand. In contrast, intermittent refrigeration dryers function in cycles, activating only when needed, which is suitable for processes with variable airflow and demand. Each type serves specific industries, ensuring optimal drying performance and energy efficiency tailored to operational requirements.</p></p>
