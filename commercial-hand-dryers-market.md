<p><ul><li>Jet Air Hand Dryers</li><li>Hot Air Hand Dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial hand dryers market primarily consists of two types: jet air hand dryers and hot air hand dryers. Jet air hand dryers use high-velocity air to remove moisture quickly, providing a fast and efficient drying experience, often with touchless operation. In contrast, hot air hand dryers release warm air to evaporate water from the hands, typically taking longer to dry. Both types offer advantages in efficiency and hygiene, making them popular choices for businesses and public facilities.</p></p>
