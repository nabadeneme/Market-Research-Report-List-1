<p><strong>The Ethernet Splitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ethernet splitter market is divided into two main types: passive and active. Passive splitters simply divide the incoming Ethernet signal into multiple outputs without any amplification, making them cost-effective but limited in range and performance. Active splitters, on the other hand, use powered devices to boost the signal, supporting longer distances and better data integrity. This distinction affects their application in various settings, from home networks to larger enterprises, catering to different performance and budget requirements.</p></p>
