<p><p>The Boiler Waste Heat Recovery System market encompasses various types, including Waterwall Heat Recovery Steam Generators (HRSG) and Cross Flow Two-Drum HRSG. Waterwall HRSGs utilize water-cooled walls to absorb heat from flue gases, enhancing efficiency and energy recovery in industrial processes. Cross Flow Two-Drum HRSGs feature a design with two horizontal drums, optimizing heat transfer by allowing flue gases to flow perpendicularly through the heat exchanger. Both systems contribute to improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions in power generation.</p></p>
