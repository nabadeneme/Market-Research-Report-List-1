<p><p>The structural glazing spacer tape market is segmented into double-sided and single-sided types. Double-sided spacer tape consists of adhesive on both sides, allowing for secure bonding between glazing materials and frames, enhancing structural integrity and thermal efficiency. Single-sided spacer tape, on the other hand, features adhesive on just one side, primarily used for fixing glass panels to a substrate or for sealing applications. Each type serves distinct roles in construction and design, catering to various glazing needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
