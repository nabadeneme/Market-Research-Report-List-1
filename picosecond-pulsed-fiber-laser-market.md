<p><p>The picosecond pulsed fiber laser market is segmented into internal trigger and external trigger categories. Internal trigger systems rely on built-in timing mechanisms for pulse generation, offering simplicity and synchronization advantages for applications like microfabrication and precision cutting. External trigger systems, on the other hand, use external signals to initiate pulse generation, providing greater flexibility and control for complex applications in scientific research and material processing. Both types cater to diverse industries demanding high speed and precision.</p></p>
