<p><strong>The Ship Radar Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>S-band</li><li>X-band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ship radar scanner market can be categorized primarily into S-band and X-band radar systems. S-band radars operate at a frequency range of 2 to 4 GHz, providing longer range detection and better performance in adverse weather conditions. They are ideal for long-range navigation and surveillance. In contrast, X-band radars operate at 8 to 12 GHz, offering higher resolution and better target discrimination, making them suitable for short-range applications like collision avoidance and traffic management. Both types are essential for enhancing maritime safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
