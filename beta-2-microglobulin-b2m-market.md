<p><p>Beta-2 Microglobulin (B2M) is a small protein found on the surface of all nucleated cells and is part of the major histocompatibility complex (MHC) class I molecules. It plays a critical role in the immune system and is involved in the presentation of antigens to T cells. Elevated levels of B2M in the blood or urine are often associated with various conditions, including renal disease, multiple myeloma, and other malignancies, making it a valuable biomarker for diagnosis and monitoring of certain disorders.</p><p>The Beta-2 Microglobulin (B2M) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases and advancements in diagnostic technologies. The rising demand for personalized medicine and targeted therapies also contributes to market expansion. Recent trends include the development of innovative B2M assays, integration of B2M testing in cancer diagnostics, and a focus on research for therapeutic applications. As healthcare systems increasingly adopt biomarkers for better patient management, the B2M market is set for significant growth and transformation.</p></p>
