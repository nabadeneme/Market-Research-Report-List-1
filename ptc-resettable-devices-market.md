<p><p>The PTC resettable devices market is primarily divided into two categories: polymer and ceramic types. Polymer PTC devices are made from conductive polymer materials, offering flexibility and lightweight designs suitable for consumer electronics and automotive applications. In contrast, ceramic PTC devices use ceramic materials, providing higher thermal stability and durability, making them ideal for industrial and high-temperature environments. Both types serve the critical function of overcurrent protection, resetting automatically once the fault condition is resolved, enhancing safety and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
