<p><p>The Light Commercial Vehicle Brake Caliper Market features two primary types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers use one piston to create braking force, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for light-duty applications. In contrast, multi-piston calipers employ multiple pistons, providing superior braking performance and heat dissipation. This makes them ideal for heavier loads and more demanding driving conditions. Both types cater to varying performance requirements in the light commercial vehicle segment.</p></p>
