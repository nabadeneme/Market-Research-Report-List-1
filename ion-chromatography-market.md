<p><p>The ion chromatography market includes three primary types: Ion Exchange Chromatography, which separates ions based on their charge and affinity to the resin; Ion Exclusion Chromatography, designed to separate ions from neutral molecules, primarily focusing on larger ions; and Ion Pair Chromatography, which utilizes counter ions to facilitate the separation of ionic compounds in high-performance liquid chromatography. Each type caters to specific analytical needs in various industries, driving advancements in analytical methodologies and applications.</p></p>
