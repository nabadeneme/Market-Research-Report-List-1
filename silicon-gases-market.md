<p><p>The Silicon Gases market includes three primary types: Trichlorosilane (TCS), Dichlorosilane (DCS), and Disilane. TCS is essential for producing high-purity silicon in photovoltaic and semiconductor applications. DCS serves as a precursor in silicon production and epitaxy processes. Disilane is used for chemical vapor deposition, providing high-quality silicon films.
