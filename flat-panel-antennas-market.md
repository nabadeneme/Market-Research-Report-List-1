<p><strong>The Flat Panel Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronically-steered Antenna</li><li>Mechanically-steered Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat panel antennas market consists of electronically-steered antennas and mechanically-steered antennas. Electronically-steered antennas utilize advanced technologies to swiftly adjust their direction without moving parts, offering enhanced performance and reliability for applications like satellite communication and wireless networks. Conversely, mechanically-steered antennas rely on physical movement to change their orientation, providing a simpler design but potentially slower response times. Both types cater to various sectors, including aerospace, telecommunications, and defense, meeting diverse operational demands with their unique steering capabilities.</p></p>
