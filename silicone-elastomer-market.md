<p><p>The silicone elastomer market includes various types such as Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR), High Consistency Rubber (HCR), Fluorosilicone Rubber (FVMQ), and others. LSR is a two-part, platinum-cured elastomer known for its flowability, precision molding, and biocompatibility, ideal for medical and automotive applications. HCR is a solid, heat-curable rubber offering high strength and durability, used in industrial and consumer goods. Fluorosilicone Rubber combines silicone's flexibility with fluorocarbon's chemical resistance, suitable for harsh environments. Other variants cater to niche applications with specialized properties.</p></p>
