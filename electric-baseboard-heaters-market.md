<p><p>Electric baseboard heaters come in two main types: fixed and portable. Fixed electric baseboard heaters are permanently installed along walls, providing a consistent heat source for entire rooms. They are typically hardwired into the electrical system and designed for long-term use. Portable electric baseboard heaters, on the other hand, are standalone units that can be easily moved between spaces. They offer flexibility for heating specific areas or when additional warmth is needed, catering to varying user needs and preferences.</p></p>
