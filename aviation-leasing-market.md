<p><strong>The Aviation Leasing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Leasing</li><li>Wet Leasing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation leasing market primarily comprises two types: dry leasing and wet leasing. Dry leasing involves the lease of an aircraft without crew or maintenance services, allowing airlines to manage operations independently. In contrast, wet leasing includes the aircraft, crew, maintenance, and insurance provided by the lessor, enabling airlines to operate flights quickly without the need for additional resources. Both leasing methods cater to different operational needs, influencing fleet management strategies and financial decisions within the aviation industry.</p></p>
