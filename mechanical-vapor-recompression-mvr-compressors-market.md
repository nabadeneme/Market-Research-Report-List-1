<p><p>Mechanical Vapor Recompression (MVR) compressors are essential in industrial applications for energy-efficient vapor recovery. Centrifugal MVR compressors utilize a rotating impeller to increase vapor pressure, making them ideal for high-capacity operations and continuous processes. They excel in applications requiring large volumes of vapor handling. In contrast, Roots MVR compressors employ a positive displacement mechanism, providing consistent flow rates and high efficiency at lower pressures. This makes them suitable for specific applications where pressure stability is critical.</p></p>
