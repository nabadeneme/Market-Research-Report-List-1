<p><ul><li>Flat Type</li><li>Slot Type</li><li>Oblique Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable dental articulators are categorized into three main types: flat, slot, and oblique. Flat type articulators feature a simple, two-dimensional design that allows for basic alignment of dental models. Slot type articulators provide enhanced stability with a slot mechanism, enabling better movement and positioning of models during procedures. Oblique type articulators are designed with an angled structure, facilitating more precise occlusal adjustments and dynamic articulation. Each type serves specific needs in dental practices, enhancing efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
