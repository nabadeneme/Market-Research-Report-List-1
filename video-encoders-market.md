<p><ul><li>Variable Bitrate(VBR)</li><li>Constant Bitrate(CBR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video encoders market encompasses two primary bitrate types: Variable Bitrate (VBR) and Constant Bitrate (CBR). VBR adjusts the data rate based on the complexity of the video content, optimizing quality while managing file size, ideal for streaming. In contrast, CBR maintains a fixed data rate throughout the video, ensuring consistent playback performance, which is crucial for live broadcasts. Both types cater to different user needs, influencing the choice of encoding methods based on application requirements and network conditions.</p></p>
