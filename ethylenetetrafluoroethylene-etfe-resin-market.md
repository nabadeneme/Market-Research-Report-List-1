<p><p>The Ethylenetetrafluoroethylene (ETFE) resin market is primarily categorized into granular and powder types. Granular ETFE is characterized by its larger particle size, making it suitable for applications that require ease of processing and high flow rates, such as injection molding. In contrast, powder ETFE has finer particles, ideal for processes like sintering and coatings, providing enhanced surface finish and adhesion. Both forms offer excellent chemical resistance, thermal stability, and durability, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
