<p><p>The textile adhesives market comprises various types, including Polyolefin (PO), Polyamide (PA), and Polyester (PES). Polyamide adhesives provide excellent adhesion and durability, often used in high-performance fabrics. Each type has distinct properties that cater to different manufacturing needs in the textile industry.</p></p>
