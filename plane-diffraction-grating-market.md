<p><strong>The Plane Diffraction Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The plane diffraction grating market includes various types based on design and function. Transmission grating allows light to pass through, while reflective grating reflects light, both used in spectroscopic applications. Sinusoidal and rectangular gratings feature different groove profiles for unique diffraction patterns. Zigzag gratings enhance resolution through a staggered groove design. Ruled gratings have precisely etched lines for optimal light manipulation, whereas holographic gratings create complex interference patterns. Copy gratings replicate original patterns for cost-effective applications in optics.</p></p>
